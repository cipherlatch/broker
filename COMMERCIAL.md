copyleft: if you run a modified version as a network service, or embed Cipherlatch
in a product you distribute, the AGPL requires you to release your
corresponding source under the AGPL as well.

If that does not work for your organization, a **commercial license** is
available. It lets you:

- embed or redistribute Cipherlatch inside a closed-source product;
- run a modified Cipherlatch as a hosted/SaaS service without publishing your
  changes;
- receive it under terms your legal team can accept in place of the AGPL.

Also available:

- **Support** — deployment help, upgrade guidance, and best-effort
  responses with a next-business-day target. Formal SLAs are negotiated
  per contract.
- **Enterprise edition** — features for regulated and hardware-backed
  deployments: HSM (PKCS#11) and cloud-KMS (AWS, GCP, Azure) signing-key
  custody, FIPS-validated and air-gapped packaging, and SIEM/audit
  export.

The free edition is not crippled: SSO/OIDC login, SCIM provisioning,
workload federation, the enforcing gateway, credential brokering, and
file- and Vault-based key storage are all included and will remain free.
Cipherlatch does not charge an SSO tax. The paid line is simple: if your keys
live in hardware or a cloud KMS, you have a compliance requirement and
this product has a price; if they live in a file, enjoy.
